What is FOSDEMx ?

FOSDEMx is a new smaller project based around the idea on having an event focussed on student level conferences. It will combine main tracks in parrallel of workshops. This edition will be focused the Python ecosystem bringing accessible conferences for every students.

Difference with FOSDEM ?

FOSDEM is about meeting the developers of around the world, we want to introduce students to the free and open source world by showing them how to use the currently available tools.
